How to use it

  • Upload a song and click Analyze Track.
  • Wait for BPM and beat markers to appear.
  • Click the waveform or drag a region.
  • Use a loop preset or resize the region.
  • Keep Snap To Beat on for beat-locked loops.

Keyboard shortcuts: Space play/pause, L loop region, S snap on/off, [ and ] jump to previous/next beat, T open/close Studio. Loop replay now uses a dedicated monitor for tighter repeats, and live visuals pulse in your teal and coral brand colors.
